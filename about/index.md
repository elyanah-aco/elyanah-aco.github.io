Welcome to my website! I'm **Elyanah Aco** from the Philippines.

I started this website because I wanted to document my own journey through tech 
without the constraints of social media and still learn some frontend stuff while doing so.

Most of the code for this website is forked from LJ Miranda's own blog [here](https://ljvmiranda921.github.io/)
with his permission. Do check him out if
you're interested in NLP architecture and machine learning research and development.

My hopes with this page, aside from me being able to record my progress
throughout the years, is to inspire others to start with their own journeys - may
it be documenting their own progress, or even starting out with tech in general.

This page contains my **personal projects and notes**. Feel free to browse around,
comment or recommend (either privately or through Github issues), and hopefully 
learn something new!

## Background

My day job is being a data analyst for a Singaporean hedge fund. Some day-to-day responsibilities
include creating and maintaining ETL pipelines for trading signal data, and conducting
research on algorithmic trading strategies. 

I obtained my bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics with a minor in Financial Economics from
the [Ateneo de Manila University](https://www.ateneo.edu/). My research interests involve natural language processing and machine learning.

Outside of tech, I also dabble in visual arts. I'm known as *gising_gising* in the local PH
art scene and primarily post my art in [Twitter](https://twitter.com/gising_gising) and 
[Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/gising_gising/). Other hobbies include playing video games,
running and reading informational websites.
